About Me

My name is Johan Jacobs and I'm the fastest man alive from Bloemfontein.

At least 18 years experience in graphic designing and 2 years experience in newspaper print production process. I am a talented, ambitious and hardworking individual, with versatile skills and have experience working as part of a team and individually. Furthermore, I am able to handle multiple tasks on a daily basis and use a creative approach to problem solving. Other key strengths are that I am always energetic and eager to learn new skills. Strong attention to detail and ability to work effectively under pressure and meet tight deadlines.


Deliver all projects in a timely fashion. I am always on time for organised events, work-related or otherwise. Always highly enthused about my work and tasks ahead and understand the importance of being interactive and enjoy communicating with others for the benefitt of the company and clients. I work with the team to provide imaginative solutions for customers. Co-workers rely on me to be on time. Even though I take my work seriously, I do have a good sense of humor. I have confidence in others and approach new challenges with an open mind.

My strengths in graphic designing are:

Communication
To communicate a client's story, brand and ideas, good communication skills. Maintain professional in tricky situations and to be clear with a client on the brief.

Curiosity
Curiosity for the world around me. Love to look beyond the surface of things, to explore both the minute detail and the big picture.

Passion and Drive
I do what I love. But even with passion and talent, design is a labour of love. The work inevitably entails odd hours, long edits, bizarre briefs and tricky workloads. Use that passion to keep going when I'm exhausted. Balance projects and master time management, and I have the drive from the start go.

Openness
Open-minded, willing to try new things and comfortable taking advice from unexpected sources. 

Ability to Take Criticism
As a designer, I am good at taking direction to improve my work and build on communication. It’s an evolving process and produces positives in the end.

Problem Solving
I think logistically and critically to make things work. Creativity is needed not only to produce something brilliant, but also to work out how the pieces fit together and turn ideas into a finished product. 

Patience
Design work can be slow going—answers don’t often come quickly. It takes patience to let an idea develop, work on communication and try out creative alternatives before getting to the end result.

Reliability
Clients can count on me. It’s important to be reliable, manage client needs, develop consistency and prove trustworthiness. It’s a sure road to a long and happy working relationship.

Evolution
I don't know it all. It’s good to keep growing and seeking new inspiration. Try new technology. Share ideas and never stop learning.
