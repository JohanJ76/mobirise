My name is Johan Jacobs and I'm the fastest man alive from Bloemfontein.

At least 19 years experience in graphic designing and 2 years experience in newspaper print production process. I am a talented, ambitious and hardworking individual, with versatile skills and have experience working as part of a team and individually.
